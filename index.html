Our Story

Founded with a passion for craftsmanship and beauty, Merchant Trading Company brings you premium imitation jewellery inspired by the rich traditions of Rajkot and Bombay.

Our skilled artisans combine traditional techniques with modern designs to create pieces that capture the essence of luxury while remaining accessible. Each item in our collection is carefully crafted to ensure quality and elegance.

We take pride in serving clients across India and beyond, offering jewellery that complements every occasion - from weddings and festivals to everyday elegance.
